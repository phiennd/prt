AMR / Biotech
2025 — ongoing
Active

FRosalind

An AI-powered antimicrobial resistance intelligence platform. FRosalind models resistance as a system — enzymatic destruction, target modification, metabolic bypass, and efflux — not just a binding-site problem. It helps doctors choose faster, more accurate treatments and improves outbreak surveillance at the hospital level.

Built on protein language models, differential geometry, and fitness landscape research. The product wedge is a mechanism-aware antibiotic ranking layer for hospital stewardship workflows — operational enough for clinicians, molecular enough to become AMR research infrastructure.
